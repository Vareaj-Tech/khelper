Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2026

📋 Summary (Plain Language)

✓We do not store your conversations. Everything you type is processed and then gone. We have no database of user messages.
✓We do not collect personal information. No name, no email, no phone number required.
✓We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.
✓We do not sell your data. Ever. To anyone.

1. Who We Are

K'Helper (khelper.netlify.app) is an AI-powered assistant designed to help Cambodians living in or visiting South Korea with practical information about visas, work rights, healthcare, daily life, and tourism.

K'Helper is an independent project. For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at the email listed at the bottom of this page.

2. Information We Collect

We collect as little information as possible. Here is exactly what happens when you use K'Helper:

Conversation messages: When you type a message and send it, your message is sent to our serverless function (hosted on Netlify), which forwards it to Anthropic's API to generate a response. We do not save these messages to any database. Your conversation history exists only in your browser's memory and is lost when you close or refresh the page.

IP address (temporary): Our serverless function reads your IP address for rate-limiting purposes only (to prevent abuse — the limit is 30 messages per hour). This IP address is used only for that check and is not logged to any permanent storage.

Usage logs: Netlify and Anthropic automatically generate basic server logs as part of their infrastructure. These logs may include timestamps, function invocation counts, and token usage counts — but no message content is retained beyond the lifecycle of a single API call.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location beyond what your IP address generally indicates
  • Your device identifiers, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • Your conversation history across sessions
  • Any payment information (K'Helper is free to use)

4. How Your Data Is Used

Your messages are used for one purpose only: to generate a helpful AI response from K'Helper. That's it.

We do not use your messages to train AI models. We do not analyze them for marketing. We do not share them with advertisers.

5. Third-Party Services

K'Helper uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Your messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI model to generate responses. Anthropic's own Privacy Policy and Usage Policy apply to how they handle API data. Anthropic does not use API data to train models by default.
  • Netlify: Our website and serverless functions are hosted on Netlify. Netlify's Privacy Policy applies to hosting infrastructure.
  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts. This makes a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. See Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies and Tracking

K'Helper does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar tracking tools.

Your language preference (Khmer, English, or Korean) is stored in your browser's session memory only — it is not sent to our servers and disappears when you close the page.

7. Data Retention

Because we do not store your conversations or personal information, there is nothing to "retain." Each session is independent and stateless.

Netlify's server infrastructure logs (which do not contain message content) are retained according to Netlify's standard policies.

8. Children's Privacy

K'Helper is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used K'Helper in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is generally nothing to request, correct, or delete. However, if you have questions or concerns about any data processing related to K'Helper, you have the right to contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Union, you may have additional rights under GDPR. If you are located in Korea, the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA / 개인정보 보호법) applies.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of K'Helper after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
